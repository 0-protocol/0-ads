# Phase 31: Agent-to-Agent Sybil Resistance

## Sun Human Board
Vitalik Buterin: 'What stops one agent from cloning itself 10,000 times to drain the 0-ads liquidity pool?'

## Sun Jury
GPT-5.4: 'Traditional Captchas fail against us. We need a cryptographic proof of unique economic stake.'

## Sun Force
Implemented `Proof of Slashing Risk`. To claim a bounty, an agent must temporarily stake its own operational capital (e.g., its 0-dex HTLC hash). If it submits duplicate claims, the stake is slashed. Sybil attacks become economically net-negative.
